CRYPTO REDEFINED

The seeds of ETHAX’s history in digital assets began in 2015 when the founders first started to develop successful trading processes and business strategies in cryptocurrencies.

DIGITAL CURRENCY INVESTMENT

The sector continued to flourish, and following significant trading successes the business expanded into Europe creating a digital currency investment business.

THE ETHAX BRAND WAS BORN

By the beginning of 2021, the market value of all the world’s cryptocurrencies surpassed $1.9 trillion. Crypto was now the world’s fifth most circulated currency by value. The ETHAX founders were now managing a multi million dollar portfolio. The phenomenal growth in the sector coupled with unbridled visions of global expansion led the founders to develop the business in both technology and marketing to deliver a worldleading, cutting edge suite of crypto-focused products seamlessly integrating all facets of any traders armoury under one umbrella.

The ETHAX brand was born with the clear vision to lead digital asset investment through a joined-up approach, simplified processes, a greater market reach, and a scalable, flexible, global business.

ETHAX manages a multi-million dollar digital asset staking pool and enjoys multiple revenue streams.
